About Astral Express

The Astral Express is a large train that serves as one of the main settings of Honkai: Star Rail. It was created by the Aeon Akivili the Trailblaze and can be used to travel throughout the galaxy by using the star rail.

The Astral Express made stops at every station, picking up rushing passengers along the way. Many continue to join and depart from the voyages of the Express. The travelers all come from different worlds, shouldering different burdens, and heading to a different destination. But once they're all on the Astral Express, they all share the same voyage experience. And that is why Himeko and the Express do not hesitate to open the doors of the Astral Express to anyone willing to share this experience, even if it means that they hold different agendas at heart, even if some were of ill-intentions.
